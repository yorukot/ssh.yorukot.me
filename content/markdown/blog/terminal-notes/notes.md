# Terminal Notes for Slow Evenings

Sometimes the best product work is not a launch, but a tiny adjustment to spacing, pacing, or feedback.

I like terminal interfaces because they reward restraint. You notice every extra line, every noisy color, and every unnecessary keybinding.
